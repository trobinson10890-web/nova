Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about NovaByte products and services

What warranty comes with my NovaByte system?

+

All NovaByte gaming PCs come with a standard 2-year manufacturer's warranty covering hardware defects and component failures. This warranty includes free replacement parts and labor. You can also purchase extended warranty options up to 5 years. Our warranty covers manufacturing defects but not accidental damage.

Can I upgrade components in my NovaByte system?

+

Yes! NovaByte systems are fully modular and designed for easy upgrades. You can upgrade your GPU, CPU, RAM, storage, and other components. We provide detailed upgrade guides for all our systems, and our support team can help you select compatible parts. We also offer professional upgrade services.

What kind of gaming performance can I expect?

+

Performance depends on your system configuration, but here's what you can expect:

  • Entry-level systems: 60+ FPS at 1080p with high settings
  • Mid-range systems: 100+ FPS at 1080p or 60+ FPS at 1440p
  • High-end systems: 100+ FPS at 1440p or 60+ FPS at 4K

These are typical expectations. Actual performance varies by game.

How much can I customize my gaming PC?

+

Complete customization! Use our PC Builder tool to select every component: CPU, GPU, RAM, storage, cooling solution, case, power supply, and more. You can also add cable management, RGB lighting, or request specific configurations. Our team is happy to help you design the perfect system for your needs.

How long does shipping take?

+

Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days after your order is processed and built. Expedited shipping is available for 2-3 business days. All orders are carefully tested and packaged before shipment. You'll receive tracking information via email so you can monitor your delivery in real-time.

Can I track my order?

+

Absolutely! Once your system ships, you'll receive an email with a tracking number and link. You can track your package in real-time through our website or your account dashboard. You'll also receive email notifications when your order is being built, shipped, and delivered.

What is your return and refund policy?

+

We offer a 30-day return policy on all systems. If you're not satisfied, you can return your PC for a full refund or exchange. The system must be in original condition with all components intact. Return shipping is covered by us. We also offer a 14-day dead-on-arrival (DOA) replacement for any defective units.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. We also offer financing options through Affirm and Klarna, allowing you to spread payments over time. All transactions are secure and encrypted.

How do I contact customer support?

+

You can reach our support team in multiple ways:

  • Live chat: Available on our website 24/7
  • Email: support@novabyte.com (response within 2 hours)
  • Phone: 1-800-NOVABYTE (Mon-Fri, 9am-9pm EST)
  • Support tickets: Create a ticket through your account

What does the warranty cover?

+

Our 2-year standard warranty covers:

  • Manufacturing defects in all hardware components
  • Component failures and malfunctions
  • Free replacement parts and labor
  • Shipping for warranty repairs

The warranty does NOT cover accidental damage, liquid damage, misuse, or normal wear and tear.

What if my PC isn't working properly?

+

Our support team will help troubleshoot any issues. Most problems can be resolved via chat or email with our experts. If you need hands-on support, we can arrange:

  • Remote assistance via TeamViewer
  • Video troubleshooting calls
  • Mail-in repairs or replacement
  • On-site service (available in select areas)

What are the recommended PC specs for gaming?

+

For 1080p Gaming (High Settings, 60 FPS):

  • CPU: Intel i5-13600K or AMD Ryzen 5 5600X
  • GPU: RTX 4060 Ti or RTX 4070
  • RAM: 16GB DDR5
  • Storage: 1TB NVMe SSD

For 1440p Gaming (Ultra Settings, 100 FPS):

  • CPU: Intel i7-14700K or AMD Ryzen 7 7700X
  • GPU: RTX 4080 or RTX 4090
  • RAM: 32GB DDR5
  • Storage: 2TB NVMe SSD

Can I overclock my NovaByte system?

+

Yes, most NovaByte systems support overclocking. We come with unlocked CPUs and GPUs that support overclocking. Our systems feature premium cooling solutions designed to handle the extra heat from overclocking. However, overclocking is done at your own risk and may void certain warranty coverage. We provide overclocking guides and support for safe overclocking practices.

How loud are NovaByte systems?

+

Noise levels depend on your configuration. Our systems typically run at:

  • At idle: 20-25dB (quiet office)
  • During gaming: 35-45dB (background noise level)
  • Under full load: 45-55dB (moderately noisy)

We offer silent builds with larger coolers and premium fans that reduce noise by 5-10dB if silence is a priority.

Can I build my PC myself instead of buying pre-built?

+

Absolutely! We also sell individual components and offer building services. Our PC Builder tool helps you select compatible parts, and we can provide guidance throughout the process. If you choose to build it yourself, we recommend:

  • Using our compatibility checker
  • Watching our assembly tutorials
  • Consulting our support team if you have questions
  • Having patience - building takes 2-4 hours

How do I know if components are compatible?

+

Our PC Builder tool automatically checks compatibility as you select components. Key compatibility factors include:

  • CPU socket matches motherboard socket
  • RAM type matches motherboard (DDR4/DDR5)
  • GPU fits in case and requires correct PSU wattage
  • Cooler doesn't conflict with RAM or case
  • Storage drives have available slots

Our tool handles all these checks for you automatically.
