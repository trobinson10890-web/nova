Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a credit card to apply? +

No! Most financing partners like Affirm and Klarna don't require a credit card. They perform a soft credit check and provide instant decisions. You'll just need basic information like your name, email, phone, and date of birth.

Will applying affect my credit score? +

Initial applications are soft credit inquiries and won't affect your score. Only if you're approved and accept the offer does a hard inquiry occur, which may have a minimal impact on your credit score.

What are the interest rates? +

Interest rates vary by partner and your creditworthiness. Many offer 0% APR for qualified buyers with specific terms. Check with each provider for personalized rates based on your credit profile.

Can I pay off my loan early? +

Yes! Most financing partners allow early payoff without penalties. In fact, paying early can help improve your credit score. Check your specific loan terms for details.

What if I'm denied? +

Don't worry! You can try other financing partners as each has different approval criteria. You can also reach out to our sales team for alternative options or payment plans.

Is my information secure? +

Absolutely. All our financing partners use bank-level security and encryption. Your personal and financial information is protected and never shared with third parties without consent.
