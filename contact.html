Frequently Asked Questions

What's your warranty coverage?

All systems come with a standard 3-year warranty covering all components. Extended coverage options are available at checkout.

How long does shipping take?

Most systems are built and shipped within 3-5 business days. Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days, express shipping available.

Can I customize a pre-built system?

Yes! Use our PC Builder to modify any component. Our team will help optimize your configuration for your needs.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. Financing options available for purchases over $1000.

Do you offer technical support?

Yes! 24/7 technical support is included with every purchase. Contact us via phone, email, or live chat anytime.

What about returns and exchanges?

30-day money-back guarantee on all systems. RMA process handled quickly with prepaid shipping labels provided.

Can you help with gaming optimization?

Our experts can optimize your system for specific games, adjust performance settings, and provide gaming tips upon request.

Do you offer educational discounts?

Yes! Students and educators receive 10% off. Bulk orders and corporate discounts also available. Contact sales for details.
