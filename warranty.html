Frequently Asked Questions

What does the standard warranty cover? +

The standard 1-year warranty covers all hardware components and labor for manufacturing defects. This includes the motherboard, CPU, GPU, RAM, storage, cooling system, PSU, and case. It does not cover accidental damage, misuse, or normal wear and tear.

Can I add a warranty plan after purchase? +

Yes, you can add an extended warranty plan within 30 days of your purchase. After that, you may still be eligible but will need to pass a hardware inspection. Contact our support team for details.

What about overclocking - is it covered? +

Damage caused by overclocking is not covered under the standard warranty. However, our Premium Pro plan includes accidental damage coverage which may cover overclocking-related failures. Always contact us before overclocking for details.

How do I submit a warranty claim? +

To submit a warranty claim, contact our support team with your order number and a description of the issue. We'll guide you through diagnostics, and if needed, arrange for shipping. Most repairs are completed within 5-10 business days.

Is liquid cooling covered? +

Yes, liquid cooling systems are fully covered under all warranty plans for manufacturing defects. Our Premium Pro plan also covers accidental damage to liquid coolers if they fail due to impact or environmental hazards.

What happens after the warranty expires? +

After your warranty expires, we still offer repair services at standard rates. You can extend coverage at any time before expiration. We also offer extended plans for out-of-warranty systems in some cases.
