About NovaByte

Engineered for Performance. Built for Gamers. Trusted by Professionals.

Our Story

NovaByte was founded in 2018 by a team of passionate gamers and PC enthusiasts who believed there was a better way to build gaming PCs. We saw a market filled with either overpriced pre-built systems or complicated custom builds that left customers frustrated.

Our mission was simple: deliver high-performance gaming PCs that are both affordable and expertly built. We started small, building systems in a garage, but our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction quickly gained us a loyal following.

Today, we've grown to become one of the most trusted names in custom gaming PCs, with over 15,000 systems built and a 98% customer satisfaction rating.
